Executive Summary

Building the Silicon Forest

This report presents the results, financial utilization, and economic impact of the national innovation program centered on the Orbtronics Innovation Hub. The program represents the early-stage development of Saint Lucia as the birthplace of the Silicon Forest — a technology-driven economic model designed to position the country as the Caribbean's leading hub for high-tech, high-growth companies. Within its first full year, the program delivered strong, measurable outcomes across talent development, entrepreneurship, digital transformation, and capital mobilization.
